Our values

  • No costumes
    We come as we are. Our different perspectives, unique
    passions, and diverse experiences bind us together. We are
    authentic, empathic, and supportive.
  • Focused curiosity
    Around every corner lies opportunity. We believe
    exploration comes before conclusions and in order to make
    a change and achieve success, we must start with curiosity.
  • Happy customer, happy life
    Our customers are our compass. We strive to understand
    the challenges small businesses face and work to make
    them smile.
  • Failure is tuition
    Creating meaningful change is risky business. In failure, we
    learn and move forward. When we stumble, we pick
    ourselves up and grow stronger, together
  • Swerve responsibly
    We are empowered to change lanes and solve problems. We
    ask for help when we need it, trusting ourselves and others
    to be accountable
  • Act ethically, always
    Choosing right is not hard, so we don’t make it difficult.
